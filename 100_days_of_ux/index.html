100 Days of UX

100 Days of UX is a learning project by Philip Adeoye where for 100 Philip learns one new design method each day.


Why am I doing this?

Two reasons: I need to learn more about the many methods employed in UX and I need to write something everyday.


What's the process?

I am reading through the highly recommended Universal Methods of Design: 100 Ways to Research Complex Problems, Develop Innovative Ideas, and Design Effective Solutions by Martin and Hannington.

While reading about each method, I make hand-written notes about what how and where each method is useful in the design process. I then do a simple Google search to find out more about the method, it's benefits, drawbacks, what to watch out for, etc.

Finally, I write about the method in my own words ...
